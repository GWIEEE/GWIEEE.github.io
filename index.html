What we do:

Workshops

In the upcomming acedemic year, whe hop to hold free workshops on circuits, programming, and more.

Events

We hold a variety of social, networking, field trips, and events with other IEEE chapters. Our flagship event is a trip to the IEEE regional conference taking place at Rowan University.

Member Benefits

Becoming a member of Gw IEEE will also grant you membership to the national IEEE orginaztion. IEEE provides internship, job, and networking opportunities and access to various other resources.
